Who I am

Hello, I’m Janelle Wen; a Product Designer based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I’ve spent the last five years solving problems through design.

Where I'm From

I have a traditional design education, with a BA in Design from the University of Southern California in Los Angeles. After working a few years in-house in entertainment and tech, I moved to Pittsburgh to pursue my Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University.

What I do

I’m well-versed in all phases of the design process, from user research, to wireframing and prototyping, to production. My experience as an in-house designer across a variety of industries has been formative to my skills in adaptibility and quick immersion.

Next Up

I am wrapping up my graduate program in August 2021. If you have an opportunity in mind that you think I’d be a good fit for after that, let’s talk!
